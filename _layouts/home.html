About Me

I'm a recent MBA graduate from Stevens Institute of Technology, where I focused on Analytics, Finance, and Strategy. I love helping organizations make smarter decisions with data, especially as they navigate AI and digital transformation.

I have nearly two years of experience as a Business Analyst at Inture Software, where I worked on financial analysis, forecasting, and building dashboards that made complex data easy to understand. At MyCaptain, I created Power BI dashboards and launched campaigns that boosted leads and revenue.

During my MBA, I worked as an MBA Consultant at Whitestone Analytics, where I led a project to reduce M&A research costs using better tech solutions. I also worked as a Teaching Assistant for Strategy courses, where I helped students dive into competitive and innovation frameworks and grew my own skills along the way. I graduated with a 3.9 GPA, earned a GMAT score of 730, and received a Provost Scholarship for academic excellence.

I'm skilled in SQL, Python, Excel, Tableau, Power BI, and financial modeling, and I'm passionate about combining data with business strategy to create real impact. I'm a member of Beta Gamma Sigma and the Graduate Consulting Club, and I'm currently exploring opportunities in finance, strategy, business analysis, or consulting.
